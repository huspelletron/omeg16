The 16th International Symposium on Origin of Matter and Evolution of Galaxies

 

 

 

 

 

 

Overview

We would like to make a first announcement for the 16th International Symposium on Origin of Matter and Evolution of Galaxies (OMEG16), which is tentatively scheduled to take place at the Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology, Hanoi, Vietnam on September 26-29, 2022. This symposium was a part of the series started in 1988. Previous OMEG symposiums have been held at Beijing, China (2015), Daejeon, Korea (2017) and Kyoto, Japan (2019).

The symposium is expected to cover a wide range of topics in nuclear physics, particle physics, astronomy, astrophysics, and earth and planetary science.

Topics to be covered:

  • Big Bang Cosmology and Primordial Nucleosynthesis
  • First Generation Stars and Galactic Chemical Evolution
  • Astronomical Observations with Light, X-Ray, Gamma-Ray, and Cosmic-Ray
  • Stellar Evolutions and Hydrostatic Burning Processes
  • Nuclear Matter and Neutron Stars
  • Explosive Stellar Objects and Nuclear Physics
  • Meteorite Analysis and Isotopic Abundance
  • Theoretical Nuclear Physics for Astrophysics
  • Experimental Nuclear Physics for Astrophysics
  • Nuclear Data for Astrophysics and Related Topics
  • Underground Nuclear Astrophysics
  • Next generation RI Beam Facilities for Nuclear Astrophysics
